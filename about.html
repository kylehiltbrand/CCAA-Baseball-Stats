Overview
What is this site?

CCAA Baseball Stats is an independent tracking project covering the Mountain, Sunset, and Ocean Divisions of the Central Coast Athletic Association in California. The site pulls game data and presents standings, individual player stats, and team stats for all 15 programs in the league.

Standard box scores exist everywhere. This site aggregates them in one place — organized by division, updated through the season, and built specifically for the CCAA.
